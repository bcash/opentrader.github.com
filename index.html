Automated Trading.

You can increase your trading opportunities with the OpenTrader Automated Trading System. You can trade faster using Expert Advisors. OpenTrader can monitor the market for you second by second and automatically handle all your trades.

Financial Forecasting.

Using financial forecasting can help business owners and managers plan future returns. The accounting or corporate finance departments may be responsible for creating forecasts.

Mobile Trading.

Stay connected and manage your trading account while on the move. Access financial markets, technical analysis and stay up-to-date with news and events without being tied to your desk.

Managing Multiple Accounts.

Are you having a hard time managing several trading accounts? OpenTrader is the ideal solution for you. Get all the tools you need for managing all your accounts in one place and at the same time!
